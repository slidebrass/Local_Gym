About Stars Gym:

At Stars Gym, we embrace and teach many different styles of fitness, because not everyone's path to their fitness goals will look the same. Every Tuesday, we will have a featured class of the week. On our home page, you will find a listing of the featured class of the week for this month.
We have several qualified, certified trainers on staff ready to help you with whatever your fitness goals are. Whatever your baseline fitness, they will tailor your training to get you to your goals. These goals might include rehabilitation from an injury, getting to a point of health and wellness that you are satisfied with, or preparing for a sporting event (just to name a few).
